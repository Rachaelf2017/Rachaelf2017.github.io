Croquembouche

Ingredients

  1. Preheat over to 475. Pipe and bake pate a choux.
  2. Fill puffs with cream.
  3. Dip puffs in melted caramel and stack in a cone shape.
  4. Drizzle melted caramel over the cone to make a web.
